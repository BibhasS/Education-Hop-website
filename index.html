Student leadership in action

Meet the organizers building Education Hop and guiding students toward meaningful service.

Ayush Kandel

President

My name is Ayush Kandel, and I attend Allen High School. I Plan to major in Finance and pursue a career that incorporates leadership, collaboration, and service. In the Leo Club, a membership based service organization, I helped organize community events and fund-raising events. What I learned from it is how beneficial volunteering is, to helping others and in helping gain essential skills. Education Hop enabled me to continue serving while inspiring other students to serve and lead by example.

Opportunities you can start right away

Simple ways to get involved, develop skills, and make an impact.

Teen Volunteer — Public Libraries

Stock shelves, prep events, help patrons, and contribute in a calm environment perfect for focus and learning.

Animal Shelters

Train, support adoption events, and assist staff while gaining experience that helps future veterinary pursuits.

Food Banks

High-impact service: sort and pack donations, collaborate with teams, and build resume-ready experience.

Arts Centers

Support instructors, lead tours, capture content, and get hands-on with community arts programming.

Religious Organizations

Distribute meals, maintain welcoming spaces, and serve visitors with dignity and care.
